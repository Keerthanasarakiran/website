About Us

At FOAM Education, we believe that every learner deserves the right guidance to reach their full potential.

Founded by Abi Russel Raj, a passionate educationist with over 2 years of experience in Student mentorship, academic planning, and International admissions, we specialize in helping students make informed, confident academic choices.

From India to Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Russia and more - we support global dreams.

Our Aim

To successfully place students for pursuing MBBS Course at an institution that is only in the best interest of the student or their profile. To promote all-round development of students abilities and personalities, and empower the potential.To provide effortless and distinctive quality service and commit to improving continually. To maintain the high standard of professionalism, performance and quality to establish exemplary excellence in higher education, and work together with prospective students and overseas colleges, universities and associate

We provide help and advice to International Students who pursue higher education abroad in Medical Field. We believe that education is a fundamental right and everyone should have access to quality higher education. With this view in mind, we strive to create opportunities for those who have genuine aspiration and honest intention, who seek excellent quality in tuition, student services, qualifications and career prospects post qualification.

Since inception, we have transformed rapidly with reputation and popularity. Repeat admission of existing students to next levels after completion of their current course of study and recommendations from them to other students have mainly contributed to our growth, and driven us to take the lead over our competitors in the geographical area that we conduct our operational activities. We have returned our gratitude to them by developing our professional skills and knowledge from training and relevant education in student counselling.
